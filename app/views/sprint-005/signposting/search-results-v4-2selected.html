Find support for veterans and their families

You can use this service to find information about support available to veterans of the UK armed forces and their families.

Filter results by location and select as many areas of support as you require. Results will include information about:

  • services that are funded by the government. These are listed first
  • charities and other non-government organisations that offer specialist support to veterans and their families.
UK veterans are defined as anyone who has served for at least one day in HM Armed Forces (Regular or Reserve) or Merchant Mariners who have seen duty on legally defined military operations

Veterans UK

The Ministry of Defence's Veterans UK helpline provides assistance on many issues including benefits, housing and welfare.

They can provide a case worker to support with multiple issues and refer to relevant organisations to support with a range of issues.

veterans-uk@mod.gov.uk

Freephone (UK only): 0808 1914 218
Telephone (overseas): +44 1253 866 043
8am to 4pm, Monday to Friday


If you need to talk to someone urgently call the Samaritans Veterans' helpline:
116 123

Samaritans also has a specialist digital service to help veterans with mental health challenges.

https://www.samaritans.org/how-we-can-help/military/samaritans-veterans-app/
