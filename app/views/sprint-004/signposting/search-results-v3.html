Use this signposting tool to find the most relevant information about services for veterans of the armed forces in the UK.

Mental health

Government services

Op Courage

Goverment service

Op Courage is an NHS mental health specialist service designed to help serving personnel due to leave the military, reservists, armed forces veterans and their families.

Get a referral to Op Courage

Charities and other organisations

Combat Stress
Combat Stress offers therapeutic and clinical community and residential treatment to former members of the UK armed forces who are suffering from a range of mental health conditions; including post traumatic stress disorder.

Help for Heroes
Help for Heroes supports veterans and serving members of the uk armed forces community with their physical and mental health, as well as their financial, social and welfare needs.

PTSD Resolution
PTSD Resolution helps veterans, reservists and families who are struggling to reintegrate into a normal work & family life because of trauma suffered during military service and other adverse life events.

Walking with the Wounded
Walking with the wounded supports veterans and their families with mental health, employment and care coordination following discharge from service.


Housing

Government services

Op Fortitude

Goverment service

Op Fortitude is a government funded service run in partnership with Riverside Housing. It was launched in September 2023 to provide supported housing to veterans who are homeless or in danger of becoming homeless.

Get a referral to Op Fortitude

Charities and other organisations

Amicus
Support for homeless veterans in partnership with the Royal British Legion.

Stoll
Provides support and access to social housing for veterans.

Royal British Legion
Offers advice and support to veterans including signposting to regional support.

Haig housing
A housing association that provides housing for veterans and their families across the UK.

Veterans launchpad
Provides accommodation and support to help veterans make a successful transition from military to civilian life.
