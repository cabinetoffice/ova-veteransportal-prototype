Finance and benefits

  1. Veterans UK

    Supports applications for compensation, pensions and welfare support for veterans and their families.

  2. Veterans Welfare Service

    Provides general help and advice to veterans or anyone supporting a veteran, their families and dependants.

  3. Defence Transition Service

    Information and support for service leavers and their families as they leave the armed forces and adjust to civilian life.

    DWP and the Armed Forces Covenant

You can use this service to request a referral to government services:

Before you start

  • we'll ask you some questions about the kind of support you need
  • we will need some personal information about your location and contact preferences
  • we will ask for your consent to share information with other government services so they understand the kind of support you need access to
