If you are not a veteran, you may still be able to use this service

If you are a family member of a veteran you may be eligible for some services yourself.

If you are applying on behalf of a veteran you can use this service on their behalf to refer them.

In both cases you will need access to this information about the veteran:

  • year of discharge
  • whether they were medically discharged
  • service number
  • name and date of birth
  • an email address to contact you or them about the referral
