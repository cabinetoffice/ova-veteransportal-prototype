Apply to be added to Find support for veterans and their families

You can apply to be added to Find support for veterans and their families if you are a:

  • charity that meets the prescribed eligibility criteria
  • statutory service run or funder by the government
  • Community Interest Company(CIC)
  • non-profit that offers support to veterans and their families
  • a statutory service - run or funded by a government body or local authority

How to apply

If you want to apply to be added to the directory, or amend a current listing, we will send you an email containing:

  • The eligibility criteria for inclusion in the directory
  • A link to a form to fill in content to be submitted for your listing
  • A 'memorandum of understanding' which sets out the terms and conditions and expectations for both the Office for Veterans' Affairs and any organisations listed in the directory
