Financial help for veterans

If you’re a veteran in the UK, there are different types of financial help available. These include:

  • grants (these are payments you do not need to pay back)
  • benefits for veterans, for example the Armed Forces Independence Payment
  • general benefits for everyone
  • discounts, for example reduced council tax payments

Grants

Grants (sometimes called ‘financial assistance’) are one-off payments. There are different types of grants available for veterans, including:

  • general grants (you can spend the money on whatever you need)
  • specific grants (you must spend the money on something specific, for example debt repayment)
  • crisis grants (you may need to prove that you have a financial emergency)
